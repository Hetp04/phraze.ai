Instructions

Step 1: Sign Up or Log In

Create an account or log in to your existing account to get started.

Step 2: Highlight Words or Phrases

Select the text you want to annotate by clicking and dragging your cursor over the desired word, phrase, or section. Once highlighted, the selected text will be ready for annotation.

Step 3: Right-Click to Open the Menu

After highlighting, right-click to open the context menu and select "Add Label" to activate the annotation extension. This will enable you to start adding detailed annotations or labels.

Step 4: Add Annotations

Add relevant labels, codes, or annotations in the format of your choice—text, voice, or video—for maximum flexibility and context.

Step 5: View Your Annotations

Easily review and manage all your saved annotations in the "Annotation History" section.

Step 6: Access Anywhere, Anytime

Enjoy seamless access to your annotations across devices by downloading the browser extension and logging in to your account.
